Lectio Continua

✦   ✦   ✦

Scripture is not meant to be visited occasionally. It is meant to be inhabited. Lectio Continua is an intensive reading plan built around that conviction — cycling through the Old Testament three times, the New Testament six times, and the wisdom literature daily, all within a single year. There are two versions: one following the full 73-book Catholic canon, and one following the 66-book canon without the Deuterocanon. Both begin at Genesis 1 and end at Revelation. Start any day. The plan has no dates.
